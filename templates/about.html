ABOUT GEOGARDEN

Geo Garden is a connective platform to support the Guerilla Gardening community. Guerilla Gardening is the act of planting and maintaining gardens on public or private property, often without the permission of the owners, with the intention of beautifying the area and creating a culture of service and good mental health.
Our team is comprised of 5 members who developed this project as the term project for CS 1530: Software Engineering.
